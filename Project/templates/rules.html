How to play the Kube

Object of the game!

To object of the game is to keep up with the Kube. The kube tells you to do these 4 commands at random.

Game Play

Smack it!

On the Kube you will find a big red button, once the kube says “Smack” you got to press the button

Twist it!

On the Kube you will find a white twisty knob, once the kube says “Twist” you got to twist the white knob any direction.

Shake it!

Grab the kube tightly, once the kube says “Shake” shake the Kube in any direction.

Cover it!

On the Kube you will find a hand with a hole, once the kube says “Cover” put your hand over the hole to cover it.

Get started

  • On the homepage you need to click on the play button.
  • On the screen will appear a small form with 2 types of games.
  • Fill in the form and choose wich game to play.
  • Get ready to have fun!

The speed game

Players: 1

Object: Keep up with the Kube and get the highest score.

Play: Respond in time to the commands you hear. Score 1 point each time you get it right, but if you’re too slow, or get it wrong, the game will end. After you can see in the leader board what your score is and how high you ranked.

As you score more points the game will get harder!

The memory game

Players: 1

Object: remember the commands you already got and get the highest score.

Play: The kube will give you the first command. Repeat the command bij using the correct sensor. The kube will duplicate the first command and adds one. Repeat these two commands by using the correct sensor. This will repeat untill you mess up! For each correct iteration you will earn a point. After you can see in the leader board what your score is and how high you ranked.
